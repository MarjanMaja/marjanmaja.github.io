GEOMGROUP

Geophysics Earth & Rock Group (GEO-M-GROUP) was established in 2023, as a non-profit organization.

  • Affirmation of geophysics, geology, and environmental sciences in society, especially in the fields of: mineral resources, geothermal energy, environmental protection, hydrogeology, construction, mining, cultural heritage, hydrocarbons, archaeology, and spatial planning;
  • Contribution to the dissemination, application, and advancement of scientific achievements and technical developments in the fields of geophysics, geology, and economic sciences;
  • Organizing presentations, conferences, symposiums, and professional events;
  • Collaboration with institutions and organizations on geophysical research, geological and environmental issues, and related disciplinary matters;
  • Collaboration with related international institutions and establishment of a regional educational center;
  • Organization of scientific and professional publications, lectures, exhibitions, etc.;
  • and a lot of more....
